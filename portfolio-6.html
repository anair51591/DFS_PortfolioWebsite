Damayanti and the Hamsa

(1899)


Damayanti and the Hamsa portrays a popular love story from the Vana Parva of Mahabharata, between Princess Damayanti and King Nala. Varma paints a scene where the Hamsa ,or the swan, tells Damayanti about Nala, and she falls in love with him. The story reveals later that the swan is actually a messenger sent by Nala
